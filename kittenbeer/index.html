Pale Ale

It’s adorable. You want to cuddle with it. You want to talk to it in a silly voice. You want to take pictures of it with shallow depth-of-field and post them on the Internet. Most of all you want to drink it. Experience the sweet, light caramel malt finish. Cherish the floral notes from the Wai-iti dry hopping. At 4.5% ABV, we don’t even feel the need to tell you to enjoy it responsibly.

Don’t fear the kitten

A Saison-style beer, brewed for the last flickering light of summer as it passes into autumn. Already the leaves are turning yellow and the days are shorter. It is a time for harvest, a time to reap what you have sown. The sun is already setting over the treetops, reminding us that just like this beer, dry hopped with a blend of exclusive South African hops, all good things will come to an end. Seasons don’t fear the kitten. Nor do the wind, the sun or the rain. You can be like them. You can learn how to fly.

Lusse-Kitten

Midwinter. Far north. Black nights reign in the absence of light. Swedes gather for the yearly celebration of lusse-fika in an attempt to fend off the darkness. At the heart of this festivity is a sweet saffron bread, known as the Lusse-kitten (lussekatt). This stout aims to harness both darkness and celebration. A midwinter's feast, saffron-infused with plenty of chocolate malt. A decadent banquet of yuletide flavours condensed into high gravity blackness - with 8% ABV, it promises to never ever go gently into that good night.
